Crystals Collector!

  • You'll be given a random number at the start of the game.
  • There's four crystals below. When you click on a crystal, you'll add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win by matching your total score to the random number.
  • However, if your total score goes above the random number, you lose.
  • The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.
  • Each time when the game starts, the game changes the values of each crystal.
